# Conditions for Becoming a Verified Seller

## Terms and Conditions

By applying to become a verified seller on ToucheArt, you agree to the following terms and conditions:

1. **Authenticity**: All products listed must be authentic and accurately described.

2. **Quality Standards**: Products must meet quality standards and be in good condition.

3. **Pricing**: Prices must be fair and competitive. No price manipulation allowed.

4. **Communication**: Sellers must respond to buyer inquiries in a timely manner.

5. **Shipping/Delivery**: Sellers are responsible for accurate delivery information.

6. **Returns/Refunds**: Sellers must have a clear return and refund policy.

7. **Prohibited Items**: Illegal, counterfeit, or prohibited items are not allowed.

8. **Account Security**: Sellers must maintain account security and not share credentials.

9. **Compliance**: Sellers must comply with all local laws and regulations.

10. **Platform Rules**: Sellers must follow all platform rules and guidelines.

## Verification Process

- Submit complete application with identity verification
- Wait for admin approval (typically 1-3 business days)
- Once approved, you can start listing products

## Violations

Violations of these terms may result in:
- Warning
- Temporary suspension
- Permanent ban

## Contact

For questions about seller verification, contact support.
